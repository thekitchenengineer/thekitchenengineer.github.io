Temperature Sensors

Most ovens today rely on digital thermocouples or resistance temperature detectors (RTDs) to track heat. These ensure more consistent cooking results than old dial-based systems.

Airflow Systems

Convection ovens use fans to circulate hot air, reducing cooking times and ensuring even browning. Some models even reverse fan direction for balance.

Smart Features

Wi-Fi connectivity, recipe databases, and AI-powered cook modes are becoming common, bridging traditional cooking with smart-home tech.
